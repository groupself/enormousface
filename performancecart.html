Performance Cart

The Original Performance Cart, 2014+
concieved as a kind of cultural vomitting, a twining homage to Deleuze and Pope L, this cart wandered the streets of NYC for years, one day pushing the length of Manhattan's Broadway from Brooklyn to the Bronx. Later itterations marched from Little Haiti to Miami Beach, and from Cougar Mountain to Seattle's Pioneer Square. Below: excerpts from a push to Maria Hernandez Park during the eclipse.
